Ekta Sethi: Best Makeup Artist in Delhi

Ekta Sethi is a passionate and dedicated makeup artist, trained under the renowned celebrity makeup artist Atul Chauhan (Atul Chauhan Makeovers, Delhi) over three years ago. With an unwavering commitment to her craft, she has transformed countless brides and clients, using only the finest international brands to ensure flawless, long-lasting results.

Every face is a fresh canvas, and Ekta's artistry brings out the most beautiful, confident version of each woman. Whether it's a bridal look, a party glow, or an editorial statement, her meticulous attention to detail and love for her work shines through every brushstroke. For Ekta, makeup isn't just about beauty — it's about unlocking the confidence every woman deserves.

  • Bridal Makeup: Complete bridal transformation — from trial to the big day, every bride becomes a canvas picture.
  • Party Makeup: Glamorous looks for sangeet, reception, or any celebration that calls for extra shine.
  • Engagement Makeup: Picture-perfect engagement looks that photograph beautifully from every angle.
